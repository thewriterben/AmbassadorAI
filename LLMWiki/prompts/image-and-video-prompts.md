# Prompt Library — Images & Video

Copy-paste prompts for image (Bing/Gemini/Firefly/Leonardo) and video (Veo/Kling/Hailuo/Pika) tools. Themes chosen to be **on-message and low-compliance-risk** (abstract/illustrative, no realistic fake people or events). → [video generation](../tools/video-generation.md) · [images](../tools/images-and-thumbnails.md)

## Visual style guide (keep it consistent)
- Mood: clean, premium, slightly editorial — "explainer," not "hype."
- Palette: gold/amber + deep navy/charcoal + white. Avoid casino/rocket/clickbait aesthetics.
- Motion: smooth, slow push-ins; no frantic flashing.
- Append to most prompts: `cinematic, high detail, soft studio lighting, shallow depth of field, vertical 9:16 composition, no text`.

## Compliance notes for visuals
- ❌ No realistic AI footage of **real people** (e.g., a recognizable official "saying" something) — risk of false attribution + platform penalties. → [video generation](../tools/video-generation.md)
- ❌ No fake "news," fake charts implying gains, rockets/"to the moon," money-rain "get rich" imagery. → [do & don't](../compliance/do-and-dont-language.md)
- ✅ Label realistic synthetic media. → [AI disclosure](../compliance/ai-disclosure.md)
