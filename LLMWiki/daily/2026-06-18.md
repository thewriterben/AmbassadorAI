## 📈 Trend radar
- **Longer is winning now.** After years of 7–15s clips, all three platforms are giving a distribution bonus to **60–90 second** videos that hold watch time. Good news for our lane — you finally have room to actually *teach* one idea. → [story structures](../craft/story-structures.md)
