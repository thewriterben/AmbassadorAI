# Participation Pathways — and Where Ambassadors Fit

The four ways people participate in DGD, and the one that defines what you do as a content-creating ambassador.

## The four pathways (WP §10.1)

| Pathway | What it rewards |
|---|---|
| **Validation** | Pre-loading funds and validating as the network grows (the main distribution mechanism) |
| **Referrals** | Introducing new participants who then validate |
| **Promotional participation** | **Posting DGD-related educational content publicly** ← *this is you* |
| **Supply-chain onboarding** | Bringing merchants and supply-chain partners into Layer-1 coin acceptance |

## You are the "Promotional participation" pathway

The White Paper defines it directly (WP §10.1):

> *"Participants who post DGD-related content publicly, including educational explanations, technical analyses, commentary on the framework, or other contributions to the public conversation about DGD, may earn DGD recognition through programs the Foundation operates. … The promotional pathway rewards the work of public discourse that grows the broader awareness of DGD."*

Note the word the White Paper uses for what you make: **educational explanations, technical analyses, commentary.** Not sales pitches. The pathway exists to grow **awareness and understanding**, not to drive purchases.

Specific recognition structures are announced through the Foundation's official channels — primarily **@DigitalGoldTalk on X** (WP §10.1). This wiki does not set or promise any recognition amounts; check official channels.

## The compliance consequence you must understand

Because the promotional pathway can pay you **DGD recognition** for posting, you have a **material connection** to the Foundation in the eyes of the FTC and the platforms. That means:

1. Your content is treated as **branded/commercial content**, and you must **disclose** it. → [FTC disclosure](../compliance/ftc-disclosure.md)
2. You're still bound by the Foundation's **[communications discipline](../compliance/communications-discipline.md)** — educational, never investment framing.
3. Platform financial-promotion rules apply to you. → [platform policies](../compliance/platform-policies.md)

This is not a reason to avoid the work — it's a reason to do it cleanly. Disclosed, educational content is exactly what the pathway is designed to reward.

## What "good" looks like for this pathway

- Explaining a Six Pillar concept clearly.
- Walking through the CFV idea or the single-price circulation argument.
- Commentary on monetary history, inflation, Austrian economics.
- Honest "here's how the design works" technical breakdowns.

## What crosses the line (don't)

- "Buy DGD before it moons." ❌ (investment solicitation)
- "I made money validating." ❌ (return claim)
- "This will hit $100k." ❌ (price projection)
- Undisclosed paid posts. ❌ (FTC violation)

See the full list in [do & don't language](../compliance/do-and-dont-language.md).

## The other pathways, briefly (so you can explain them accurately)

- **Validation** is the core engine — see [supply & distribution](supply-and-distribution.md).
- **Referrals** reward bringing in participants who validate (WP §10.1).
- **Supply-chain onboarding** is the multi-year work of getting merchants → wholesalers → manufacturers → raw-material suppliers to accept qualifying Layer-1 coins, with cascading recognition at each link (WP §10.2). It's how the "Adequate Circulation" pillar gets realized in the real economy.
