## Two honest realities about "free"

1. **Free tiers shift monthly.** Credits, watermarks, resolution caps, and even tool names change. Every tool page is tagged `verified: 2026-06`. Treat specifics as "last known," and confirm on the tool's site.
2. **Watermarks matter for cross-posting.** A watermark from one app can get your video down-ranked or rejected on another platform. Prefer no-watermark free exports where possible (noted per tool).

## Recommended "all-free" starter stack (intermediate creator)

> **Script:** ChatGPT/Claude/Gemini free → **Voice:** ElevenLabs free (label it AI) → **Visuals:** Veo via Gemini or Kling free + Bing/Gemini images → **Edit & captions:** CapCut → **Music:** YouTube Audio Library / Pixabay → **Schedule:** native platform schedulers or a free Postiz/Buffer tier.

Full step-by-step: [workflows.md](workflows.md).

## Golden setup rules

- **Keep a `/raw` folder** of your generated clips, voiceovers, and images so you can reuse and re-cut. (Mirrors the wiki's raw-sources idea.)
- **Caption everything** — most short-form is watched on mute.
- **Export vertical 1080×1920** unless a platform needs otherwise. → [platform specs](../craft/platform-specs.md)
- **Disclose AI voices/avatars and your sponsorship** every time. → [AI disclosure](../compliance/ai-disclosure.md) · [FTC](../compliance/ftc-disclosure.md)

## A note on tool choice and the message
No tool excuses a bad script or a compliance slip. The [communications discipline](../compliance/communications-discipline.md) governs the *content* no matter how it's made. Great tools + safe, educational message = your edge.
