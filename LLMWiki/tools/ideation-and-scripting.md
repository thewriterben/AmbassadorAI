# Step 1 — Ideation & Scripting (Free AI Writers)

Turn a DGD concept into a tight 30–60 second script. AI used purely for **writing** does **not** require AI disclosure (it's production assistance) — but the [communications discipline](../compliance/communications-discipline.md) still governs every word.

## Free tools

| Tool | Free tier (Jun 2026) | Best for |
|---|---|---|
| **ChatGPT** (chatgpt.com) | Free tier with capable default model | Scripts, hooks, outlines, rewrites |
| **Claude** (claude.ai) | Free tier | Long, nuanced explainers; careful tone |
| **Google Gemini** (gemini.google.com) | Free tier; integrates with Veo video | Scripts + ideas in the Google stack |
| **Microsoft Copilot** | Free | Quick drafts, integrated with Bing images |

Any one is enough. Use them to **draft**, then you **edit for accuracy and compliance**.

## The critical workflow rule

**Always feed the AI the guardrails.** Generic AI will happily write "invest now, it's going to the moon." Prevent that by pasting the compliance frame into your prompt every time. Use the ready-made prompts in [script-prompts.md](../prompts/script-prompts.md) — they include the discipline.

## A minimal good prompt (expanded versions in the prompt library)

```
You are scripting a 40-second educational short-form video for a Digital Gold (DGD) ambassador.
HARD RULES (never break):
- Educational only. NOT investment advice.
- Do NOT call DGD an investment, predict price, promise returns, or say "buy/get in early."
- Describe ideas and design, never financial outcomes.
Topic: [e.g., "Why money loses value over time"]
Audience: curious general public, some social-media users, no economics background.
Deliver: (1) a 1-second scroll-stopping hook, (2) 4–6 short spoken lines, (3) an on-screen text version of each line, (4) a non-salesy CTA to "read the white paper / follow to learn more."
Keep sentences short. Conversational. One idea per line.
```

## Always do after the AI drafts

1. **Fact-check against the wiki.** Cross-check claims against [dgd/](../dgd/dgd-overview.md). The AI may invent numbers — your White Paper figures are the source of truth.
2. **Run the do-and-don't pass.** Scan for banned words. → [do & don't language](../compliance/do-and-dont-language.md)
3. **Add disclosures** if you have a material connection. → [FTC](../compliance/ftc-disclosure.md)
4. **Tighten the hook.** The first line earns the next. → [hooks](../craft/hooks-library.md)

## Script length cheat-sheet
- 15–30s: one idea, one hook, one payoff. Best for TikTok/Reels.
- 30–60s: a small "explainer arc." Great for YouTube Shorts and X.
- ~120 words ≈ ~45–50 seconds of natural narration.
