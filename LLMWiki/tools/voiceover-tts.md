# Step 2 — Voiceover & Text-to-Speech (Free)

Turn your script into clean narration. **Realistic AI voices must be disclosed** as AI on every platform. → [AI disclosure](../compliance/ai-disclosure.md)

## Free tools

| Tool | Free tier (Jun 2026) | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **ElevenLabs** (elevenlabs.io) | ~10,000 credits/mo (≈10 min of speech). **No commercial license on free** — and free/ signed-out content **must attribute "elevenlabs.io" in the title.** Commercial use requires a paid plan. | Most natural voices; great for explainers. Since ambassador content is rewarded (commercial), the free tier's no-commercial-license terms are a real constraint — use your own voice or a paid plan for sponsored clips. |
| **Microsoft Edge "Read Aloud" / Edge TTS** | Free, unlimited via browser | Surprisingly good neural voices; screen-record or use edge-tts |
| **Google Gemini / Cloud TTS** | Free tier credits | Solid neural voices |
| **CapCut built-in TTS** | Free inside the editor | Convenient; voices are decent, not the most natural |
| **TTSMaker / TTS Vibes / AnySpeech** | Free, MP3 download, many no sign-up | Quick, lots of voices incl. the "TikTok voice" |
| **Your own voice** | Free | Often the best for trust; AI just cleans it up |

## Two honest recommendations

1. **Your real voice + AI cleanup beats a fake voice for trust.** For financial-adjacent education, authenticity converts. Record on your phone, then de-noise in CapCut. No AI-voice disclosure needed if it's genuinely you.
2. **If you use an AI voice, pick a natural one and LABEL it.** A tiny "AI voice" on-screen note + the platform toggle keeps you compliant and honest.

## Quality checklist for narration
- **Pace:** slightly faster than conversational; short-form rewards energy.
- **Pauses:** add a beat after the hook and before the payoff.
- **Loudness:** normalize to about -14 LUFS; consistent volume across clips.
- **De-noise:** kill background hiss (CapCut → Audio → Reduce noise).
- **Pronunciation:** check terms like "Hayek" (HY-ek), "Menger" (MENG-er), "Mises" (MEE-zes). Type phonetic spellings into the TTS if needed.

## Licensing gotcha (read this)
Free tiers often grant **personal** use but require a **paid plan for commercial use** or require **attribution**. Since ambassador content tied to a reward is commercial, check each tool's commercial-use terms. Edge Read Aloud and your own voice avoid the issue entirely.

## Workflow
1. Paste final, compliance-checked script.
2. Generate narration; download MP3/WAV.
3. Drop into [CapCut](captions-and-editing.md) and auto-generate captions from it.
4. Add "AI voice" label if synthetic. → [AI disclosure](../compliance/ai-disclosure.md)
