# Verification Report — 2026-06-23

Weekly re-verification of time-sensitive facts: free AI-tool tiers and social-platform policies. Searches run the week of 2026-06-23. **Net result: ~8 facts changed, all toward *stricter* disclosure or *tighter* free tiers.** The single most important update for ambassadors this week: **Google Veo's free export now carries a visible "Made with Veo" watermark** — and separately, **TikTok and YouTube now auto-detect and auto-label AI content**, so self-disclosing first is now the only safe play.

## What I checked
- **Free-tier tools:** Google Veo / Gemini / Flow / Vids, Kling, Hailuo, Pika, Pixverse, ElevenLabs, CapCut, Bing Image Creator, Adobe Firefly, Leonardo, YouTube Audio Library, Pixabay, Uppbeat — credits, watermark, resolution, commercial-use.
- **Platform policies (2026 changes):** TikTok, Instagram/Meta, YouTube, X — crypto/financial promotion, branded-content disclosure, AI-generated-content labeling.

## What changed (old → new)

### Tools
1. **Google Veo — watermark (video-generation.md) — BIGGEST TOOL CHANGE.**
   - *Old:* "No watermark on free Veo exports."
   - *New:* Current model is **Veo 3.1**. Free generation via **Google Flow** (~50 daily credits ≈ 10–12 clips/day, 720p, 8-sec clips) now carries a **visible "Made with Veo" watermark on free *and* Pro** (removed only on the $249.99 Ultra plan). **Google Vids exports remain watermark-free** — route through Vids for a clean export, or crop/cover. All Veo output also carries invisible SynthID.
   - *Impact:* Ambassadors who relied on clean free Veo exports must switch to Google Vids or crop the mark before cross-posting (a visible watermark can get a clip down-ranked on other platforms).

2. **Bing Image Creator — commercial use (images-and-thumbnails.md).**
   - *Old:* "Personal; check MS terms for commercial."
   - *New:* Free outputs are now **explicitly restricted to non-commercial personal use** under Microsoft's terms — **do not use on rewarded/sponsored ambassador content.** (Bing now also offers MAI-Image-2e, GPT-4o, and DALL·E 3 models.) Recommended commercial-safe free swaps: Gemini/Imagen, Leonardo, or Firefly.

3. **Adobe Firefly — IP indemnification nuance (images-and-thumbnails.md).**
   - *Old:* "Commercially safe outputs" (lowest legal risk).
   - *New:* Still trained on licensed/public-domain data and commercial use is allowed on free, **but Adobe's IP indemnification (legal defense if a claim arises) is a paid feature** — free users don't get that backstop. Reframed from "commercially safe" to "lowest *content-source* risk."

4. **ElevenLabs — free-tier specifics (voiceover-tts.md).**
   - *Old:* "Monthly free character credits; commercial use on paid, attribution on free."
   - *New:* Pinned down the numbers: **~10,000 credits/mo (≈10 min)**, **no commercial license on free**, and free/signed-out content **must include "elevenlabs.io" in the title.** Flagged that rewarded ambassador content is commercial, so the free tier is a real constraint — use your own voice or a paid plan for sponsored clips.

5. **CapCut — pricing + watermark nuance (captions-and-editing.md).**
   - *Old:* "No watermark on most exports."
   - *New:* Plain manual exports of your own clips are still clean, **but a watermark appears with Pro-locked templates or certain AI-generated clips.** Noted the **2026 pricing restructure (Standard $9.99 / Pro $19.99)**. Added "check the preview before exporting."

6. **Pika / Pixverse — minor refresh (video-generation.md).**
   - Pika free still exports watermark-free **but now runs the older Pika 1.x model** (latest needs paid). Pixverse clarified to **~90 credits/day, no watermark** (one of the few clean free exports). Hailuo clarified to ~2–3 clips/day at 768p.

### Platform policies (all *tightened* — compliance pages strengthened, never weakened)
7. **TikTok (platform-policies.md, ai-disclosure.md).** Commercial-content disclosure is now **mandatory for any brand/product/service post**; a paid partnership needs **two** disclosures (Branded Content toggle **and** an AI label). TikTok now **auto-detects AI via C2PA and auto-applies a "Contains AI-generated content" label** even without self-disclosure. Crypto promotion remains limited to licensed/regulated entities via application.

8. **Instagram/Meta (platform-policies.md, ai-disclosure.md).** **AI-content disclosure is now mandatory for all advertising/boosted content, applied globally** (EU AI Act–driven). Crypto-lending/BNPL pulled into the **Credit Special Ad Category**; financial ads with non-standard risk-warning text are rejected (must use Meta's templates).

9. **YouTube (platform-policies.md, ai-disclosure.md).** Policy refreshed Jan 2026; **as of May 2026, automatic AI detection can label synthetic content even if you don't.** Reassurance added: **disclosing as altered/synthetic does not reduce reach or monetization.**

10. **X / Twitter (platform-policies.md, ai-disclosure.md).** **"Smart Cashtags" have now launched** — a `$TICKER` can render live price charts and links to outside exchanges in-timeline (X is not a broker / no trade execution). A **pre-share AI-content warning prompt** is rolling out. Strengthened the price-discipline warning since posts may render beside live price + a trade button.

11. **EU AI Act (new cross-platform note).** Transparency rules requiring **machine-readable AI labels become fully applicable 2 August 2026** across platforms operating in the EU. Added to both compliance pages and the universal-takeaways list.

## What stayed the same
- **Kling** ~66 daily credits, watermark on free — unchanged.
- **Leonardo** ~150 tokens/day, commercial allowed on free — unchanged.
- **Gemini/Imagen** ~15/day, commercial permitted, no watermark — unchanged.
- **Music libraries (music-and-sound.md):** YouTube Audio Library (mostly no attribution; some CC-BY), Pixabay (CC0-style, no attribution, commercial OK), Uppbeat (free tier requires credit) — **all unchanged; no edit needed.** (Opportunity noted below re: Uppbeat.)
- **Core line in the prime directive:** educational, never investment framing — untouched and reinforced.

## New risks & opportunities for ambassadors
- **Risk — clean free exports are shrinking.** The reliable watermark-free free options for AI *video* are now narrower: **Google Vids (not Flow), Pika, Pixverse.** A stray "Made with Veo" mark can get a clip down-ranked when cross-posted. Always preview-check for a watermark before export.
- **Risk — auto-labeling means you can't "skip" AI disclosure.** TikTok and YouTube will label your AI content for you if you don't — and being caught not self-disclosing is worse than the label. Self-label, every time.
- **Risk — free tiers vs. "commercial" reality.** Because ambassador content is rewarded (commercial), **Bing (personal-use-only)** and **ElevenLabs free (no commercial license + forced attribution)** are now poor fits for finished posts. Prefer your own voice, Gemini/Imagen, Leonardo, or Firefly.
- **Opportunity — disclosure is genuinely free.** YouTube confirms AI/altered labels **don't** hurt reach or monetization, so honest labeling carries no cost and builds the credibility that financial-adjacent education depends on.
- **Opportunity — Uppbeat cross-platform whitelisting.** Uppbeat clears tracks for YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, and Facebook, which is handy for multi-platform cross-posting (free tier still requires the on-track credit).
- **Watch item — X Smart Cashtags.** Educational DGD posts may now render beside live price data and a trade link. Keep price-talk discipline airtight; never let a post read as a buy/return signal.
