# Ready-to-Shoot Scripts — "Is It a Security?" & "Casino vs Money"

Two ready-to-shoot mini-series in one pack. **Theme A** explains the safe-harbor / digital-commodity idea; **Theme B** is the anti-degen "boring on purpose" angle. All compliance-safe. Each episode ~30–40s. Produce with any [workflow](../tools/workflows.md); finish each at the [pre-publish checklist](pre-publish-checklist.md).

> **Disclosure on every episode** (you have a material connection): spoken in first ~3s + on-screen + caption-front. Reusable block:
> - Spoken: *"Heads-up — I'm in Digital Gold's ambassador program, so this is sponsored and educational, not financial advice."*
> - On-screen (0–3s): **SPONSORED · EDUCATIONAL · NOT FINANCIAL ADVICE**
> - Caption start: **#Ad — educational, not financial advice.**

> **Two compliance rails specific to this pack:**
> - Theme A: "safe harbor" is **legal** — never let "safe" slide into "safe investment / safe haven."
> - Theme B: you may use degen lingo to hook attention; **never** market DGD as a degen play ("ape in," "100x," "prints"). → [do & don't](../compliance/do-and-dont-language.md)

**Series format:** consistent 1.5s intro card per theme; outro card "Read the white paper · Follow to learn more." Palette gold + navy. Captions burned in.
