# Video Brief Template

Fill one of these before producing. Copy the block, paste into your notes, complete it. It keeps you fast and compliant.

```
─────────────────────────────────────────
DGD VIDEO BRIEF

Title (working):
Series / standalone:        [e.g., Six Pillars #1 of 6]
Core idea (ONE sentence):
Topic source (wiki page):   [e.g., dgd/six-pillars.md — Scarcity]

AUDIENCE & GOAL
Audience:                   [general public / crypto-curious / etc.]
What they learn:            [the single takeaway]
Why they'd share it:        [the "send this to a friend" reason]

PLATFORM(S)
Primary:                    [TikTok / Reels / Shorts / X]
Length target:              [e.g., 35s]
Aspect/spec:                1080×1920, 9:16

SCRIPT
Hook (≤1s):
Structure:                  [Hook→Gap→Payoff→Loop / Myth→Reality / etc.]
Spoken lines:
On-screen text:
CTA (educational only):     [follow to learn more / read the white paper]

VISUALS
Style:                      gold + navy, editorial, no hype imagery
B-roll / images needed:     [list]
AI tools:                   [Veo / Kling / Bing / Firefly...]
Title card text:

AUDIO
Voice:                      [my voice / AI voice — if AI, must label]
Music:                      [library + track + license note]

COMPLIANCE (pre-fill, verify at end)
[ ] Educational only, no investment framing
[ ] FTC disclosure plan (spoken + on-screen + caption + toggle)
[ ] AI disclosure plan (if realistic synthetic media)
[ ] Facts checked vs white paper
[ ] Banned-word scan planned

POST DETAILS (per platform)
Caption (disclosure FIRST):
Hashtags:
Title (YouTube, searchable):
─────────────────────────────────────────
```

## How to use
1. Fill the brief (5 minutes).
2. Produce via a [workflow](../tools/workflows.md).
3. Finish at the [pre-publish checklist](pre-publish-checklist.md).
