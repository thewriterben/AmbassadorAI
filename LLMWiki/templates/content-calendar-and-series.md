# Content Calendar & Planning

A simple, sustainable planning system. Consistency beats intensity — pick a cadence you can keep.

## Weekly cadence template (4 platforms)

| Day | Topic | Format | Primary platform | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Mon | Inflation 101 stat | Number→Breakdown | Shorts + repost all | searchable title |
| Tue | Pillar of the week | Hook→Gap→Payoff | TikTok + Reels | series #n |
| Wed | Myth-buster | Myth→Reality | X + TikTok | drives comments |
| Thu | Glossary term | ELI12 | Reels + Shorts | 20–25s |
| Fri | History / thinker | Then vs Now | Shorts + X | story-driven |
| Sat | Behind-the-scenes / Q&A | Talking head | wherever you're strongest | trust-builder |
| Sun | Rest / engage / plan next week | — | — | reply to comments |

Adjust down to 3×/week if needed — just keep it **regular**.

## Monthly view (run a series per ~2 weeks)
- Weeks 1–2: **"6 Rules of Real Money"** series (Ep 0–7). → [series ideas](series-ideas.md)
- Weeks 3–4: **"Money, Explained"** standalone shorts + 1 myth-buster series.
- Always-on: react to relevant news with educational angle (never price-hype).

## Planning ritual (20 min/week)
1. Pick the week's theme/series.
2. Draft a [video brief](video-brief-template.md) per slot (or batch-script with [prompt #3](../prompts/script-prompts.md)).
3. Batch-produce ([Workflow C](../tools/workflows.md)).
4. Schedule; tailor caption + disclosure per platform.
5. Review last week's analytics (CTR, replay, share) and double down on winners.

## Simple tracking sheet (copy into a spreadsheet)

```
Date | Title | Series/Ep | Platform(s) | Hook | Format | Disclosure done? | AI label? | Views | Replays | Shares | Notes
```

## Repurposing rule
One core video → 4 platform cuts (tailor length/caption/disclosure; satisfy the **strictest** platform). → [platform specs](../craft/platform-specs.md) · [platform policies](../compliance/platform-policies.md)

## Scheduling tools (free tiers)
- Native: YouTube Studio, Meta Business Suite (IG/FB), TikTok scheduler, X scheduler.
- Cross-platform free tiers: Postiz, Buffer. Mind [X automation rules](../compliance/platform-policies.md) — no spammy duplicate posts.
