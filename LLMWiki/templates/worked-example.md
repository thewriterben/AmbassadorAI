# A worked example: one video, start to finish

Every artifact for a single finished episode, so you can see exactly what "done" looks like
at each step of [the complete pathway](the-pathway.md). Topic: **why your money covers a
little less each year.**

## 1. The brief
- **Topic:** Inflation & debasement
- **Takeaway:** money covers a little less each year because more is created over time
- **Audience:** general public, new to this
- **Hook type:** Contrarian Claim
- **Format:** faceless explainer, vertical 9:16
- **Platforms:** TikTok, Reels, Shorts, X

## 2. The script (4-column)

| Time | Spoken | On-screen text | Visual |
|---|---|---|---|
| 0–3s | "Your money covers a little less each year — and it's by design." | NOT FINANCIAL ADVICE | title card |
| 3–18s | "Here's the mechanism: when more money is created than the goods it can be exchanged for, each unit holds a little less purchasing power." | More money, same goods → less purchasing power | eroding stack of notes |
| 18–35s | "A printing press has no fixed limit, so the value of each note quietly slips. That's inflation, working exactly as the system is built." | No fixed limit = quiet erosion | banknote fraying to dust |
| 35–50s | "Digital Gold takes the opposite route: fixed, transparent rules instead of a printer you can't see." | Fixed, transparent rules | glowing network, no center |
| 50–60s | "Follow to learn how sound money is meant to work — the white paper has the full picture." | Follow · read the white paper | vault opening to soft light |

## 3. The shot list
1. Title card (gold/navy) — *"Why your money covers less each year"*
2. Eroding stack of notes (b-roll)
3. Single banknote fraying to dust (b-roll)
4. Abstract glowing network, no center (b-roll)
5. Vault door opening to soft light (b-roll)
6. Lower-third strip: *"Not financial advice · Educational"*

## 4. The captions

**X (≤280 characters):**
> Your money covers a little less each year — by design. More money, same goods, less purchasing power. Digital Gold uses fixed, transparent rules instead of a printer. Follow to see how sound money works.
> Not financial advice. Educational.

**TikTok / Reels / Shorts:** the same caption, plus
`#soundmoney #economics #monetarypolicy #austrianeconomics #cryptoeducation`

## 5. The disclosures
- **On screen, 0–3s:** "NOT FINANCIAL ADVICE" (add "#Ad" if you're recognized for posting; add "Made with AI" if you used a realistic AI voice).
- **Caption:** lead with #Ad if sponsored; end with "Not financial advice. Educational."
- **Platform switches:** branded-content toggle on if sponsored; AI label on if realistic AI media.
