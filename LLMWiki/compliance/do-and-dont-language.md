# Do & Don't Language — The Phrase Cheat-Sheet

Pin this next to your editing setup. Left column = rewrite or delete. Right column = safe alternative.

## ❌ Banned / 🔁 Rewrite

| ❌ Don't say | ✅ Say instead |
|---|---|
| "DGD is a great investment" | "DGD is a monetary system designed to work like sound money" |
| "It's going to $100k / to the moon" | "The price advances along a fixed curve as the network grows" |
| "Get in early before it rises" | "Here's how the distribution is designed to work" |
| "You'll make money / passive income / ROI" | "No one earns income from operating the network — fees are burned by design" |
| "Guaranteed returns" | "Nothing here is guaranteed; it's a reasoned design, and the white paper says so" |
| "I made $X validating" | "Here's how validation is designed to work, mechanically" |
| "Don't miss out / last chance" | "If the idea interests you, the white paper explains it in full" |
| "This will replace the dollar" | "This is proposed as a competing private currency, in Hayek's sense" |
| "Financial freedom / retire on this" | "A design aimed at preserving purchasing power" |
| "Trust me, it's the next Bitcoin" | "Here's how its design differs from Bitcoin's" |
| "Buy now / link to buy in bio" | "Read the white paper / official channels in bio to learn more" |

## Words to avoid entirely
invest, investment, investor, returns, ROI, profit, gains, passive income, moon/mooning, 100x, pump, early, get in, don't miss out, guaranteed, risk-free, financial freedom, retire, the next [Bitcoin/Ethereum], price target, undervalued, cheap, deal.

## Words that are safe and on-brand
money, currency, monetary design, sound money, purchasing power, scarcity, circulation, distribution mechanism, protocol, fixed rules, Austrian economics, framework, valuation method, educational, mechanics, design, white paper, decentralized.

## Topic-specific traps

### "Safe harbor" (legal term — never "safe investment")
| ❌ Don't say | ✅ Say instead |
|---|---|
| "DGD is a safe harbor — a safe place for your money" | "DGD is designed to stay *outside securities law* — that's what 'safe harbor' means legally" |
| "Safe harbor means you can't lose" | "Safe harbor is about regulation, not your wallet" |
| "Regulators approved DGD" | "DGD is *designed to align* with the framework; it's a reasoned position, not an approval (WP §12.16)" |
| "It's a safe haven asset" | "It's designed as a *digital commodity* — money, not an investment haven" |

> Remember: calling DGD a profit play actually **attacks** its digital-commodity status, which requires value to come from the protocol "rather than from expectations of profit." → [safe-harbor page](../dgd/positioning-safe-harbor.md)

### "Degen" (speak to the culture — never sell a degen play)
| ❌ Don't say | ✅ Say instead |
|---|---|
| "Ape into DGD" / "the next 100x gem" | "Tired of the casino? Here's a different philosophy of money" |
| "This is the degen bag that prints" | "DGD is the *anti*-degen coin — boring on purpose" |
| "Don't be exit liquidity — buy this instead" | "Don't be exit liquidity — *understand* what money is" |

> You may use degen lingo to **hook attention and contrast**; you may not use it to imply gains. → [positioning & audiences](../craft/positioning-and-audiences.md)

## The "rewrite reflex"
Whenever a sentence implies **the viewer will be financially better off**, stop and rewrite it to describe **how the system works** instead. Same facts, different frame.

- Before: "Coins are released as users join, so getting in early means more coins." ❌
- After: "Coins are released as users join and split among all current users, so the per-person share shrinks as the network grows." ✅ (mechanics, no profit implication)

## Visual & on-screen text counts too
The rules apply to **captions, on-screen text, thumbnails, hashtags, and AI voiceover** — not just what you say out loud. A clean script with a "🚀 TO $100K" thumbnail still violates the discipline.
