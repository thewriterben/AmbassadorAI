# Compliance Overview — Read Before You Post Anything

Three rulebooks govern every video you make. Break any one and your content gets removed, demonetized, or worse — and it can create legal exposure for you and the Foundation.

## The three rulebooks

1. **The Foundation's communications discipline** — *educational, never investment framing.* This is the most important and the most specific to DGD. → [communications-discipline.md](communications-discipline.md)
2. **Platform Terms of Service** — each of TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, and X restricts financial/crypto promotion and requires AI-content disclosure. → [platform-policies.md](platform-policies.md)
3. **The law (FTC + securities)** — if you're rewarded for posting, you must disclose it; and you must not promote DGD as an investment. → [ftc-disclosure.md](ftc-disclosure.md)

## Why this matters more for DGD than for a normal product

DGD's entire legal posture is that it is **not a security** (WP §12). That argument depends partly on the **consistency of public statements** with the design — the White Paper commits the Foundation, its officers, and its channels to marketing discipline (WP §12.11). As an ambassador in the **[promotional pathway](../dgd/participation-pathways.md)**, your public posts are part of that record. A single ambassador saying "DGD is a great investment, it's going to $100k" undermines the structural argument for everyone.

So the discipline isn't bureaucratic caution — it's load-bearing.

## The one-question test

Before any line of script, ask:

> **"Am I teaching an idea, or am I selling an outcome?"**

- Teaching an idea (how inflation works, what the six pillars are, how the CFV formula measures value) → ✅ allowed.
- Selling an outcome (you'll profit, it'll rise, get in early, passive income) → ❌ never.

## The disclosure layers (when you're rewarded for posting)

| Layer | What | Where |
|---|---|---|
| Material connection (FTC) | "Sponsored / ambassador" | spoken in first seconds + on-screen + caption |
| AI content (platform) | "Made with AI / synthetic" | platform toggle + on-screen when realistic |
| Not-advice (good practice) | "Not financial advice; do your own research" | spoken + caption |
