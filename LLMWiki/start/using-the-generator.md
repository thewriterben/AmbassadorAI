# Using the idea generator

The **Idea generator** (top of the menu, under "Create") is your fastest route from blank
page to a usable starting point. Everything it produces is built from the approved
playbook, so it's safe by design — you just write the final words.

## The three tools

- **🎬 Video idea** — one click gives a full mini-brief: a topic, the angle, a ready hook, who it's for, the format, and a matching visual. Hit **Regenerate** for a new combination; **Copy brief** to grab it.
- **🪝 Hooks for a topic** — every hook style, filled in for one topic, so you can pick the opening line that grabs you. **New topic** to reroll.
- **✍️ Prompt set** — copy-paste prompts for an image, a video clip, a voiceover style, and a full script — already matched to your topic. Paste these into your AI image/video tool or any chat.

## How to actually use it

1. Open **Idea generator** and spin **Video idea** until one excites you.
2. Want options for the opening line? Check **Hooks for a topic**.
3. Grab the **Prompt set**: paste the script prompt into an AI chat for a first draft, and the image/video prompts into your free tools.
4. Take it into [make your first video](your-first-video.md) from Step 2.

## Good to know

It **recombines** a curated set of safe topics and hooks — fast and always on-message, but
it won't invent brand-new claims (that's the point). If you ever need genuinely
open-ended drafting, the local creator tools include an AI writer that checks every line
against the rules automatically before handing it back.
