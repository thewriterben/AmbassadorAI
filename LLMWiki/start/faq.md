# FAQ — common beginner questions

**Do I have to show my face?**
No. "Faceless" explainers with abstract b-roll work great. A real face builds extra trust, but it's optional. → [end-to-end workflows](workflows.md)

**I have no budget. Can I really do this for free?**
Yes — every step has a $0 tool. → [the free AI toolkit](toolkit-overview.md)

**Can I use an AI voice?**
Yes, but label it as AI if it sounds realistic. A real recorded voice needs no AI label and builds the most trust. → [disclosures explained](disclosures-explained.md)

**How long should videos be?**
Short — usually 30–60 seconds, one idea. → [platform specs](platform-specs.md)

**What am I actually allowed to say?**
Explain how the system is designed; never promise anyone will be financially better off. → [the rules in plain English](what-the-rules-mean.md) and [approved talking points](approved-talking-points.md)

**What's the difference between "safe harbor" and a "safe investment"?**
"Safe harbor" is a legal term — DGD is designed to qualify as a digital commodity, not a security. It does **not** mean a safe place for money, and you must never use it that way. → [safe harbor](positioning-safe-harbor.md)

**Do I need to disclose anything if no one pays me?**
You always add "Not financial advice." Add #Ad only if you're recognized or rewarded for posting. → [disclosures explained](disclosures-explained.md)

**Where do I get ideas when I'm stuck?**
The Idea generator (menu → Create), the [six pillars](six-pillars.md), or [series ideas](series-ideas.md). → [using the generator](using-the-generator.md)

**What if I'm not sure a line is okay?**
When a sentence hints the viewer will come out ahead, rewrite it to describe the mechanism instead. The do/don't list is your friend. → [do / don't language](do-and-dont-language.md)

**How often should I post?**
Sustainable beats sporadic — pick a rhythm you can keep (say, 3 a week) and plan ahead. → [content calendar & series](content-calendar-and-series.md)

**What do I do after posting?**
Notice what landed and make the next one a little better. → [the complete pathway](the-pathway.md)
