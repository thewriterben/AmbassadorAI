# The video wizard: what it does, and what you get

The [Video wizard](#wizard) (top of the menu, under **Create**) is the fastest way to go
from "I want to make a video" to a finished, ready-to-film plan — without learning any
tools first. It asks a handful of plain questions, checks every line against the rules as
you go, and hands you a complete plan you can copy or download. It even remembers your
progress, so you can stop and come back later.

## What it asks

One short question at a time, with a progress bar:

1. Your idea (pick one, hit *Surprise me*, or type your own)
2. The single thing the viewer should understand
3. Audience, format, and platforms
4. Your hook (pick one or write your own — checked live)
5. Your script (hook → gap → payoff → loop — checked live)
6. Your voice (your own, or AI)
7. Disclosures (recognized for posting? AI voice?)
8. Your visuals (matching prompts to copy)

Tip: in the **Idea generator**, the *Use in wizard* button drops a generated idea straight
into the wizard so you can skip step 1.

## What you get at the end

A single plan, copy- or download-ready, with a green / amber / red compliance badge:

```
# Your DGD video plan - Inflation & debasement

## Brief
- Topic: Inflation & debasement
- Takeaway: how money covers a little less each year as more is created
- Audience: the general public (new to all this)
- Format: Faceless explainer
- Platforms: TikTok, X

## Script (film this)
| Time  | Spoken | On-screen | Visual |
| 0-3s  | Most people misread inflation. Here is the mechanism they miss. | NOT FINANCIAL ADVICE | title card |
| 3-20s | Most people blame shops. The real story is the money supply.    | (key phrase) | banknotes eroding into dust |
| 20-45s| When more money is created than the goods it can be exchanged for, each unit holds a little less purchasing power. | (key phrase) | banknotes eroding into dust |
| 45-60s| Follow to learn how sound money works. | Follow / read the white paper | network awakening |

## Captions
### X
How money covers a little less each year as more is created. Follow to learn how sound money works.
Not financial advice. Educational.

## What to do now
1. Record the voiceover in your own voice.
2. Make the b-roll with the prompts above (free tools).
3. Make a title card with your headline.
4. Assemble in CapCut and burn in captions.
5. Put the disclosures on screen in the first 3-5 seconds.
6. Run the pre-publish checklist.
7. Post or schedule to: TikTok, X.
```

That is the whole video, planned. Open the [wizard](#wizard) and try it — about two minutes
from blank slate to a plan you can film.
