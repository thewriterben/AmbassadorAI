# AI Video Trends & Styles Radar

Auto-generated by the **trend radar** scheduled task (`dgd-ai-video-trend-radar`) and used
by the [DGD Video Studio agent](../../skills/dgd-video-studio/SKILL.md) (Job D). Newest at
the top. Each report covers the latest AI-video tools/models, trending short-form formats
and hooks, emerging visual styles — and, for every trend, **how to ride it educationally**
without breaking the [communications discipline](../compliance/communications-discipline.md).

> A trend only counts if the *educational* version still works. We borrow structure and
> style; we never adopt "buy / pump / safe-investment" framing. Mechanism, not forecast.

## How to use
Skim the newest report → pick a trend with a DGD topic tie-in → take it into the
[DGD Video Studio](../../skills/dgd-video-studio/SKILL.md) walkthrough or a
[workflow](../tools/workflows.md) → finish at the [pre-publish checklist](../templates/pre-publish-checklist.md).
